Scanner/Printer
The library offers scanners in the Digital Lab for preserving your old snapshots or documents. After scanning, use the Mac to edit and save your treasures.
The scanners are:
1. Epson Perfection V600 Photo Scanner (featuring 6400 x 9600 dpi for enlargements up to 17″ x 22″).
2. Epson FastFoto FF-680W High Speed Photo Scanner with Feeder Tray (featuring up to 1200dpi; scans items up to 8″ x 10″ and up to 35 at a time).
If you would like to scan directly to email without editing, the Library’s copier on the main floor includes this feature.
