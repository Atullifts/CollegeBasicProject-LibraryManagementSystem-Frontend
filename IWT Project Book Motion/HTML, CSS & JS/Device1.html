Digital Labs
The Digital Lab is an advanced multimedia creation center available to Book Motion cardholders. Whether you need to compose or edit a musical masterpiece, take photos for Events, record a podcast, shoot hi-def video, or scan cherished family snapshots, we’ve got you covered.
The Digital Lab is equipped with a variety of tools for digital creation including a keyboard, microphone, tablet, photography studio equipment and more. If you are interested in more information about the Lab’s features, please call the Library or Email(Provided in Contact Us)
The Digital Lab is available for Book Motion cardholders in 3 hour increments.
